High Concept

The player must grow their own colonies to defend themselves and destroy their opponents.

Genre

Action Strategy

Platform

Universal

Story

As a germ, the player must show their supremacy by defeating both the body they're invading and other germs.

Aesthetics

A more abstract representation of the body and germs themselves are employed in this game, supported by ambient sound and light sound effects.

Gameplay

Using the mouse or touch control, the player will grow their germs in colonies automatically, then send those germs to attack and capture other colonies, or attack the host. This simple control scheme will be taught in a tutorial that acts as the first level.
